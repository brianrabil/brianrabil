## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** Most AI products are either vaporware demos or enterprise tools that take forever to ship. Founders and teams need someone who can build real, usable AI products quickly.
**Why alternatives fall short:**
- Agencies are expensive and slow
- Most AI "experts" have theory but haven't shipped products
- Generic developers don't understand AI-native product patterns
**What it costs them:** Months of wasted development time, products that feel like AI was bolted on, missed market windows.
**Emotional tension:** Fear of building the wrong thing; frustration with AI hype vs. reality gap.

## Competitive Landscape
**Direct:** Other AI product developers / developer portfolios — most are less prolific or less product-focused.
**Secondary:** AI development agencies — fall short on speed, cost, and founder-level product thinking.
**Indirect:** Hiring a full-time AI engineer — fall short on breadth of experience and speed to start.

## Differentiation
**Key differentiators:**
- Ships consistently (15+ products in portfolio across live, building, and backlog stages)
- Full-stack: product thinking + design + engineering + AI integration
- Goldman Sachs enterprise experience combined with fast, independent execution

**How we do it differently:** Rapid discovery, high-signal prototyping, iterative release with instrumentation at every step.
**Why that's better:** Products get to market faster with real usage signals driving decisions, not assumptions.
**Why customers choose us:** Combination of product craft, technical depth, and shipping velocity that's rare in one person.
